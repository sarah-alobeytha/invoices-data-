Tax_number: Seller’s taxpayer ID.
Invoice_number: Unique invoice identifier.
Issue_date: Unix timestamp [2022-2025]
Collection_type: payment type [CASH, CREDIT]
Invoice_sub_type: Invoice subtype ["LOCAL", "EXPORT", "AREA_OF_DEVELOPMENT"]
Invoice_category: invoice category ["INCOME", "GENERAL_SALES", "SPECIAL_SALES"]
Invoice_discount_amount: discount amount.
Invoice_tax_amount: Tax amount.
Buyer_identification: Buyer’s taxpayer ID.
Buyer_name: Buyer's name.
Days_overdue: Days beyond due date that payment was late (0 if on time).
Expected_payment_amount: Final expected amount.
